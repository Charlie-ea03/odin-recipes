Carbonara Recipe

Homepage

Description

This classic Italian dish brings a nostalgic yet proffessional flavour to you dinner table. And it's so easy to cook, all it takes is 5 minutes prep time, and 10 minutes cooking time. Making this dish perfect for when you are in a rush, home late from work, or just fancy a quick, low effort dish.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs (Yolk)
  • Guanciale or Pancetta
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Parmasan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
